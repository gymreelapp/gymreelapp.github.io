How it works

From the gym to
your grid in minutes.

01

Log your session

Start from a template or blank. Add exercises, log sets with weight and reps. Auto-fill pulls from your last session so you know exactly where to pick up.

02

Hit your PR

Personal best detection fires the moment you log a new max — with haptic feedback so you feel it in real time, even mid-set.

03

Design your post

Pick a photo, drag your stats card to the perfect spot, and preview exactly what your followers will see before it goes live.

04

Share the proof

Post to the Gym Reel feed or push straight to Instagram Stories. Your stats are baked into the image — no screenshots, no overlays falling off.

GPS tracking

Run data as serious as your training.

Live route mapping, real-time pace, elevation gain, and calorie burn. Audio cues announce your split pace at every mile so your phone stays in your pocket.

  • Real-time distance, pace, and elevation via CoreLocation
  • Audio pace announcements via AVFoundation
  • Treadmill manual-entry fallback
  • Editable title and notes after your run
  • Battery-optimised — GPS revoked immediately on stop
