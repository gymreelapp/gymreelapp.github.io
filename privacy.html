Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Gym Reel ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you use Gym Reel, we may collect the following:

  • Account information — name, email address, and username provided during sign-up (including Sign in with Apple).
  • Workout and run data — exercises, sets, weights, reps, GPS routes, pace, distance, and duration that you log in the app.
  • Photos and media — images you choose to attach to posts. These are stored in Firebase Storage.
  • Location data — GPS coordinates collected during an active run session only. Location access is revoked immediately when a run ends. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as app crashes and performance metrics collected via Firebase.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Gym Reel service.
  • To display your posts and workout data on your profile and in the social feed.
  • To calculate personal bests, progress graphs, and run statistics.
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase.
  • To send you relevant in-app notifications (you can disable these in iOS Settings).

Data Storage and Security

All workout data is stored locally on your device first using SwiftData, and synced to Firebase (Google Cloud) when an internet connection is available. We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules to ensure only you (and users you explicitly share with) can access your private data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is shared only as follows:

  • Firebase / Google — our backend infrastructure provider. Data is processed under Google's privacy policy.
  • Instagram — if you choose to share a post to Instagram Stories, the image is passed to the Instagram app on your device via iOS share sheet. We do not send data to Instagram's servers directly.
  • Other users — posts marked "Public" are visible to all Gym Reel users. Posts marked "Friends only" are visible to your followers. Posts marked "Private" are visible only to you.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Gym Reel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or by updating the effective date above.
